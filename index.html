    Shawn fell in love with web development in mid-2021, and he is currently completing the "Full Stack JavaScript Techdegree" program at Team Treehouse, a coding education platform.

    Shawn's background is in music composition, performance, and education. As a teacher, he has worked with thousands of children and led over 20,000 classes over a 17-year period.

    Shawn's musical compositions have received wide regard, having been performed and recorded in the United States, Europe, and Japan.

    As a performer, Shawn has appeared with GRAMMY® award winners Charlie Daniels, Kenny Rogers, and Doc Severinsen, and he was seen on international television as part of George W. Bush's second innaugration parade.

    Shawn lives near Denver with his wife, Whitney Kelley, and two daughters. He graduated summa cum laude with a Bachelor of Music in Music Education from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, in 2008.
