  • About

      Shawn Kelley fell in love with web development in mid-2021, and he is currently completing an immersive Full-Stack JavaScript Developer program with Team Treehouse, a coding education platform.

      Shawn's background is in music education, performance, and composition.

      As a teacher, Shawn has worked with thousands of children and led over 20,000 classes in a 17-year period.

      Shawn's original works have received international recognition. His first piece, Urban Suite, has been recorded, performed, and featured accross the world.

      Shawn has appeared with GRAMMY®-award winners Doc Severinsen, Charlie Daniels, and Kenny Rogers. He was also seen on international television when he performed in President Bush's second innauguration parade.

      Shawn is a perpetual learner and loves to read non-fiction. He lives near Denver with his wife, Whitney Kelley, and two daughters.
